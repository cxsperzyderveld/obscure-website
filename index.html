About Obscure

Obscure is a digital manipulator — a rogue genius who uses advanced code and AI to reshape the fabric of reality itself. Born from trauma, he sees the world not as it is, but as broken code — and he’s rewriting it in his own image.

His Enemies

  • Clarity – His idealistic future self, trying to undo the damage he caused.
  • Null – The analog professor, sworn to disrupt Obscure’s digital reign.
